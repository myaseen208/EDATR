5.1 Example 5.1 (Pg. 68)

Example 5.1 (Pg. 68)

In 1985 species/provenance trials were laid out at six sites in Thailand as part of an ACIAR project extending over several years to investigate Australian multi-purpose tree species. The experimental design in each case was an RCB design with three replicates and the number of seedlots ranged from 30 to 42. Plots consisted of \(5 \times 5\) trees with a $2m m $ spacing. Plot summary files were constructed for the 24-month. measurement according to the methods described in Chapter 3. Analyses were performed on the plot mean data at each site. Further details are given by Williams & Luangviriyasaeng (1989).
